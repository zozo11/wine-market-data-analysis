Datasets originally and Purpose¶

This dataset is public available for research. The details are described in Kaggle https://www.kaggle.com/zynicide/wine-reviews, The last updated is 8 month ago, should be early 2018. I belive the owner of this dataset scraped all the information on twitter. The purpose of this datasets analysis is to discuss the impact of New Zealand wine product on the globe market. In this rearch i will look at integrate information on global wines, including variety, origins, points and price, and then analyze whether New Zealand wines differences with international wine development.

summary¶

This data set comes from the web crawler in twitter that include wine rating, price, region, winery and so on information, and there are many imperfections. I hope to find the advantages and disadvantages of New Zealand wine through the above data. From the sample data, it can be seen that the United States is still a big country in wine consumption and production, with high-end and medium-end wine as the mainstay, while France as the old wine producing area still accounts for the score and price. New Zealand is a new wine exporter. Its history is not as long as France, Italy and other countries, but it has a unique natural advantage, the wine winery is spread all over the country, and the main wines grown and brewed are in line with international fashion, mainly based on Sauvignon Blanc, Pinot Noir, Chardonnay and mixed wines, also she is very promising in the future.

During this research i will discuss those questions:¶

  1. Genuinely view of the proportion of global wine consumption and production.
  2. Understand the price of global wines and the relationship between prices and points especially in NZ wine points and price.
  3. Observe the main popular wine varieties and the main varieties of New Zealand wine, and consider whether New Zealand wine is in line with international mainstream.
  4. Find the proportion of New Zealand wine in global market, through dataset samples, analyze geographic location and the potential of New Zealand wineries.

Description of attributes:

Wine review:

  1. country: The country that the wine is from
  2. description: A few sentences from a sommelier describing the wine's taste, smell, look, feel, etc.
  3. designation: The vineyard within the winery where the grapes that made the wine are from
  4. points: The number of points WineEnthusiast rated the wine on a scale of 1-100 (though they say they only post reviews for wines that score >=80)
  5. price: The cost for a bottle of the wine
  6. provinceThe province or state that the wine is from
  7. region_1: The wine growing area in a province or state (ie Napa)
  8. region_2: Sometimes there are more specific regions specified within a wine growing area (ie Rutherford inside the Napa Valley), but this value can sometimes be blank
  9. taster_name: Name of the person who tasted and reviewed the wine
  10. taster_twitter_handle: Twitter handle for the person who tasted and reviewed the wine
  11. title: The title of the wine review, which often contains the vintage if you're interested in extracting that feature
  12. varietyThe type of grapes used to make the wine (ie Pinot Noir)
  13. wineryThe winery that made the wine
